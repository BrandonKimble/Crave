# Verdict: Austin-insider voice, dish-category claim; founder channel zero 60–90 days; locality-first sourcing; first paid deal = $300–500/mo trials that gate into a "Crave ATX" anchor account — no astroturf, ever

## The call

**Identity: hybrid, with a precise division of labor.** The _content voice_ is the Austin insider; the _claim_ is always the dish-category claim. Every hook names the city and the dish ("the #1 birria in Austin is X — fight me"); the category positioning ("the app that ranks dishes, not restaurants") lives in store metadata and the pinned comment, never as the hook. This is ledger/01 conflict 2's own resolution — category = vertical, geography = executed through hooks and creator identity — and neither brief disputed it.

**Formats, ranked by the measured conversion ladder** (ledger/05, Flame's 0.05–0.2% / 0.3–0.5% / 0.5–1% view→download):

1. **Demo-wow screen recording** of ranked dish search (top rung, 0.5–1%; passes Kelechi's "show me something I haven't seen" — nobody has seen dish-level city rankings).
2. **Rankings-as-controversy, receipt-backed** ("Austin's most overrated restaurant, per 40,000 Redditors") — Matt/Jenny's engineered 50/50 divide occurring naturally, product named intrinsically because the score IS the claim.
3. **Programmatic listicle slideshows** (0.05–0.2%) — built as _software_ (auto-generated Top-N from live Score + evidence quotes), run Noise-style as the cheap retargeting layer, never the growth engine. Build it only after formats 1–2 validate distribution.

Operating protocol: 5–6 formats in 2 weeks; gates = ~15k views flags a candidate, 3-sec watch-through ≥70–75%, skip <20%, and above all "what app is that" comment density; triage <300 = account, 300–1k = format, 1–5k = keep format vary copy; cross-post IG + TikTok + FB; CTA in pinned comment, never spoken; patience budget 25 videos / 100–200 days before a winner.

**Sourcing doctrine: locality first, charisma second.** The pool is Austin food/lifestyle creators (dozens, not thousands); within it apply the charisma/comment-density/audience-geo screen. Charisma-first national scouting is rejected — Crave's buyer is not everywhere.

**First paid deal (day ~60–90): $300–500/mo trial retainers for 4–8 videos/mo to the top 1–2 scouted Austin creators, on their own accounts**, on the convergent grid: $20–50/video-equivalent base + non-stacking milestones ($60/$200/$500/$800 at 20k/100k/500k/1M, single-platform trigger), 20/80 payment split, perpetual usage rights, no hard cap on a winner. Judged by the identical gates as founder content, plus Austin-resident view share.

**Anchor-creator bet: yes, but only as a gated conversion of a passed trial (~day 90–150), never an upfront concentrated commitment.** A trial creator who clears the founder-content gates AND has a majority-Austin/central-Texas audience converts into the zero-follower "Crave ATX" account (StudyFetch's mechanic scaled down) at $500–1,500/mo cash retainer + the same non-stacking milestones; optional promo-code revenue kicker; **no equity**. If nobody clears, dollars default to a 3–5-creator roster on the standard grid. The roster is additive volume _after_ the anchor or a validated format — never the identity.

**Engagement-bait line:** Allowed — receipt-backed claim controversy (outrage attaches to the _claim_, evidence in-post), seeded debates about real ranking calls, pinned CTA comments _from the brand or creator's own identifiable account_, asking the audience "did we get this wrong?" Prohibited — planted comments from fake or sock personas (including the Cal AI plant-and-pin from a fake user), paid undisclosed commenters, manufactured outrage/rage-bait, fake demos, undisclosed AI personas, disguised-discovery scripting for paid creators. The test is ledger/05 Conflict C: outrage at the claim is marketing; outrage at a deception is brand poison for an evidence-receipted product.

**Spark geo-amplification** (verified DMA/metro-level, crave-fact-sheet.md): amplify organic winners at the Sideshift gate (ER ≥7.5–8% → ~$20 boost, kill <5%) aimed at the Austin DMA. This is the answer to the one-city geo-dilution objection, and it is small dollars.

## Why

- **Founder-first carried on mechanism, not echo.** 12+ sources across three channels, but the load-bearing reasons are independently derived: you cannot vet/brief/coach creators without personal viral literacy, and receipts unlock recruiting (ledger/05 consensus 1, ledger/06 consensus 1). The counter-cases (Glow, Clear30) resolve as category mismatches — pain-driven products with generic formats — while food is repeatedly named content-native.
- **The two briefs converged on 80% of the doctrine**; the real dispute was sequencing weight. The anchor brief's original "skip the diffuse grid, make one concentrated bet" **died in its own pre-emption section** — it retreated to "run the same screening funnel, trial 1–2, then commit depth." That retreat is correct: power-law variance (1-in-85 creatives win; 2,000x view spread on the same script three days apart) makes an unvetted single pick indefensible. What survives is the anchor _as the escalation path_, which I adopt — because the Austin pool is dozens (relationship depth beats spray), identity compounds behind one face where it cannot across a dozen $20/video deals, and the premium-trust literature (ledger/01 conflict 3) says a hard-paywall premium product needs a trust persona, with "one deep local-creator partnership" as the named bootstrap scale-down.
- **The StudyFetch citation is real and independently doubled** — spine/03 and ledger/06 converge on the identical Crave transfer ("Crave ATX," $500–2,000/mo local pricing) without cross-reference. Verified against spine/03 directly.
- **The factory-as-growth-engine framing died to its own numbers**: Copia's 13M views → ~0.1%, Jack Frics's 20x demo-vs-slideshow contrast. The "receipts-on-slide beats the corpus baseline" claim is an untested hypothesis the founder+factory brief itself flagged as unverifiable; it ships as a gated experiment budgeted at meme-tier conversion.
- **Equity for the anchor died**: every equity enthusiast in the corpus is an equity holder (ledger/06), and a solo bootstrapped cap table has no room for a marketing hire's 50%.

## Discarded as noise

National view benchmarks (1.2B/500M/400M) — global-TAM survivor telling; Pingo's Russia case proves geo-mismatched virality is spend, not growth. Vendor-claimed farm economics (Noise's $0.08 installs, Sideshift's earnings claims) — sales pitches. Phone farms, warmed personas, VPN fleets — ToS-adverse and trust-toxic. Cluely's mindshare-over-conversion — VC-subsidized. Charisma-anywhere doctrine (Jenni) — assumes the buyer is everywhere. Fake-student/disguised-discovery scripting — the named saturation-and-trust-poison pattern. StudyFetch's $10k/mo price level — post-raise capital, not the mechanic.

## Reversal triggers

1. **Two consecutive 2-week founder cycles at sub-300 views across formats** → Marcus Burke's niche-throttle confirmed even in the founder's hands; the whole creator machine (anchor included) is void. Shift to community seeding (r/austinfood, local FB groups), local press, and paid geo-targeting.
2. **Live Austin Spark test leaks reach heavily outside the DMA** → geo-amplification lever gone; creator-identity becomes the only geo filter; factory downgraded to negligible.
3. **No scouted creator has a real, affordable, meaningfully-Austin audience** → the StudyFetch analog collapses ("a zero-follower account nobody follows over"); default to the 3–5 roster grid.
4. **Trial creators fail the gates founder content passed** (no watch-through lift, no "what app is that" density beyond Brandon's own) → the persona isn't earning a premium; standard grid only.
5. **A second/third trial creator clearly outperforms the chosen anchor** → concentration was the wrong shape; widen to the small roster.
6. **Factory with receipts converts at the generic ~0.1% floor** → reclassify as awareness spend, stop investing engineering in it.

## Owner-conditional items

- **Timing vs launch blockers**: channel zero cannot convert until Apple enrollment/paywall/legal URLs are live; posting earlier builds waitlist + receipts. Brandon sequences founder-hours against the launch checklist.

- **Promo-code revenue kicker** for the anchor requires attribution plumbing (App Store offer codes) — owner decides if the build is worth it vs flat retainer + milestones.
- **Austin-resident audience threshold** for anchor conversion (suggested ≥50% Texas-plausible) — owner tunes against real analytics.
