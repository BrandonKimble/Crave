## Bottom line

**The section faithfully represents the raw data.** Every load-bearing headline
number I checked digit-by-digit came back CONFIRMED — Massive (40–50K / 0.5% / 8M),
Runafy (3,000 / 32% / 20%), Pingo (~200K / ~half-of-23K), PrayScreen (70 listings /
2–3 mo), the Blue Throne bands (50/35–50/<20 and 50/40/30/20), Lobby (25–30% vs
half; 60 vs 20), Alberto (1-in-6..10 vs 1-in-30–35), the Symmetry quote, Payout
($9,700 / 5 creators), Cardstock ($4.99 / 12 / $60), StudyFetch (80% / $100-per-100K
/ $50–2,500), Sideshift ($5–8 → sub-90¢), Zoom U (40K / 7:1), Fitted (100K / 2M),
Nomad Table (12–15K → 6K / ~20). Consensus #4 (win-one-geography), which is the
spine of the blueprint's Austin-only call, is the single best-supported claim in the
section and is carried cleanly across four sources. No fabricated numbers, no
invented sources.

**The defects are accuracy blemishes, not decision-breakers:**

1. **DISTORTED — Payout "Food & Drink chart placement"** (recommended-shape prose).
   Raw and the ledger's own numbers table both say **Finance**. Fix the prose to
   "Finance-category chart placement" — the current wording falsely implies a
   direct precedent in Crave's exact App-Store category.
2. **DISTORTED — the fake-scarcity "sold out with 20/100 seats" anecdote is
   misattributed to "PrayScreen."** It's the superwall--20251009 restaurant-marketing
   founder, a different episode. Drop "PrayScreen's" (or re-label). Discard-pile item,
   but it reveals two founders were conflated.
3. **OVERSTATED — consensus #3 "two independent founders, same verdict."** YourMove
   ("press → ~0 installs, value = SEO backlinks") and Flame ("media presence ≠ PMF")
   make related-but-distinct claims. The blueprint §10 line "press for installs (SEO
   backlinks only)" is really a one-source (YourMove) finding; Flame is looser support.
   Don't bank it as two independent corroborations.
4. **OVERSTATED — blueprint §10 "Pingo 400M views."** Founder's own conservative
   figure is 350–370M ("I don't know yet"); 400M is the host hook. Downgrade to
   "~350–400M." (This ledger's numbers table correctly avoided 400M; only the
   blueprint prose over-reached.)
5. **Over-precise (minor):** Sideshift "hands-on for the first 1–2 months" (raw says
   only "in the beginning"); Pingo "~23K peak new **iOS** users" (raw shows Android in
   frame, garble acknowledged); Jay's "best creator in his own network" quote not
   found verbatim (substance confirmed).

**What the blueprint should change:**

- Fix the Payout category (Finance, not Food & Drink) and the PrayScreen
  misattribution.
- Soften §10's Pingo view count to ~350–400M.
- When §4 cites the conversion ladder (0.5–1% / 0.05–0.2%), caveat that those rates
  are **phone-farm-derived** (Flame, 200 posts/day) — the ordering transfers to
  Brandon's channel-zero, the volume does not; and confirm whether the "~300-view
  organic-thesis-fails" tripwire is borrowing Flame's shadowban diagnostic.
- Weigh **Stronger's 12-month first-mover window** against PrayScreen's 2–3 months in
  §1 — the clock risk is currently drawn from the fastest example only.
- Surface **Nicole's +50% (hard paywall beats let-them-poke-around)** in §2 as the
  concrete magnitude of the corpus doctrine the pre-wall demo is knowingly defying.
- Optionally add **Stronger and Nicole as founder-first sources** (consensus #2) — both
  are clean, first-party, and currently missing.
