**This section is a faithful representation of the raw data.** Every load-bearing number that feeds
the blueprint — Blue Throne's category rule + $20 ChatGPT reset + $30M/500k/125k payer math + year-1
resubscribe 50/40/30/20 (§8) + top-20%-engagement retention curve; the PrayScreen 2-3mo/70-clone

the hook-wording-as-audience-filter mechanism (§1) — was located in the raw and verified digit-for-
digit or with the ledger's own honest hedges. Auto-caption garbles (Flo/"flow", Runna/"Runner",
Strava/"Straa", General/"German" Atlantic) were correctly decoded, not mis-transcribed.

**Three fidelity defects, all minor and none touching a blueprint call:**

1. **`$6.5M of VC` (Sunflower, Conflict 3) — NOT-FOUND.** The figure is not in the cited transcript,
   which supports only "VC/YC-funded" + a career-wide "$10-20M on influencer marketing". Recommend
   striking the specific number or tagging it "external/unverified".
2. **`~$0.25/download` (Fitted, Conflict 4) — DISTORTED.** Raw says 1-15¢/download and frames it as a
   POSITIVE (cheap acquisition) about the founder's apps generally, not a Fitted cautionary cost.
   Recommend correcting to "1-15¢/download" and dropping the "cautionary tale" spin (or replace with
   the raw's actual cautionary signal — Fitted hamstringing growth behind its hard paywall).
3. **Jay/Nomad "algorithm can't hold a geography" (Conflict 2) — OVERSTATED.** Jay's refusal is real,
   but his stated reason is a mobile TRAVELER audience, not a general algorithmic law. He is a weaker
   analog for geo-anchored Crave than presented.

**What the blueprint must change: essentially nothing load-bearing.** Two optional refinements:

- **§1 wording:** the flat assertion that store positioning "can't out-argue an algorithm that won't
  hold a city" is over-leveraged on the Jay/Nomad case. It still stands on the US-creator→US-audience
  evidence (`superwall--20260524`, CONFIRMED) and Pingo's Russia lesson (CONFIRMED), and the fact
  sheet's own verified finding that TikTok/Spark CAN geo-target by DMA (which blueprint §6 already
  uses). Soften "won't hold a city" to "won't hold a city _organically_" for internal consistency
  with §6.
- **§4 citation:** add a one-line clarifier that the "Noise two-pronged model" borrows only the
  content STRUCTURE — Noise's engine (account farms, comment-seeding, disguised discovery) is exactly
  what §4/§10 prohibit.

**One tradeoff to put in front of the owner (not a defect):** the blueprint's blanket ban on
transaction-abandoned / decline-cascade re-offers (§2/§10) forecloses a tactic a verified $500k/mo
operator (Pingo) uses profitably. The compliance rationale is sound for a gate-everything app, but
it is a real revenue tradeoff, not a free lunch.
