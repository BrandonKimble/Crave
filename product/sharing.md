# Universal sharing (owner locked 2026-07-11)

One share surface for the whole app — registry §8.2 carries the details.

- THE UNIVERSAL SHARE MODAL: invocable anywhere; shows a beautiful
  preview of the share package as the recipient sees it; context options
  (invite-as-collaborator for own lists); destinations = Crave DMs +
  friends (universal closeness sort) then external platforms (iMessage,
  Instagram stories/DMs, WhatsApp, Telegram, X, email, Messenger,
  Snapchat, TikTok, LinkedIn, Facebook feed/stories — research pass owed
  on each API).
- SHAREABLE OBJECTS + deep links: lists; restaurants; dish cards (open
  the restaurant profile deep-linked at the dish); polls; comments (open
  the poll scrolled + highlighted); user profiles.
- THE SHARE PACKAGE: per-object rendered card (ranked previews for
  lists), adaptable to stories formats, link previews, and the /l/{slug}
  web landing (get-the-app CTA) — the landing page IS the package. Crude
  v1 now; beauty pass owner-in-loop later.
- Closeness sort = ONE algorithm reused for friend ordering everywhere
  (share sheet, friend clusters, collaborator stacks). To be designed.
